About

I am a computer science researcher working on computer graphics and neural rendering. I received my M.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from POSTECH in 2025, where I was advised by Prof. Seungyong Lee in the Computer Graphics Lab. My M.S. thesis was on High-Quality Geometry and Texture Editing of Neural Radiance Fields.

Research Interests

  • Neural rendering & NeRF — geometry and texture editing, mesh-based neural rendering.
  • 3D reconstruction — depth completion from RGB-D and sparse depth video.
  • Geometry processing — remeshing, mesh saliency, and surface editing.
  • View synthesis — view-dependent texture and appearance modeling.
